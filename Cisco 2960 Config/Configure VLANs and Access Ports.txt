Objective: Segment the switch into two separate broadcast domains (VLANs) for "Sales" and "R&D" departments.

Steps:

1.Create the VLANs.
Creating and naming VLANs makes them easier to manage.

SW4(config)# vlan 10
SW4(config-vlan)# name SALES
SW4(config-vlan)# exit
SW4(config)# vlan 20
SW4(config-vlan)# name R&D
SW4(config-vlan)# exit


2.Verify VLAN Creation.
SW4# show vlan brief

You will see VLAN 1 (default) and your new VLANs 10 (SALES) and 20 (R&D). All ports are in VLAN 1 by default.


3.Assign Single Ports to an Access VLAN.

We will put port FastEthernet 0/1 into the SALES (VLAN 10) VLAN.
SW4(config)# interface fastEthernet 0/1
SW4(config-if)# switchport mode access
SW4(config-if)# switchport access vlan 10
SW4(config-if)# exit


4.Assign a Range of Ports to an Access VLAN.
SW4(config)# interface range fa0/13 - 24
SW4(config-if-range)# switchport mode access
SW4(config-if-range)# switchport access vlan 10
SW4(config-if-range)# exit


5.SW4(config)# interface range fa0/25 - 36
SW4(config-if-range)# switchport mode access
SW4(config-if-range)# switchport access vlan 20
SW4(config-if-range)# exit


Save Your Configuration.
SW4# copy running-config startup-config
or
write


Verification Commands:
1.show vlan brief: You will now see ports Fa0/1, Fa0/2-10 assigned to their respective VLANs.
2.show interface fa0/1 switchport: Shows detailed switchport information for a specific port.
